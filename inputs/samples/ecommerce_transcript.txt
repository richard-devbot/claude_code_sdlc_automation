Meeting Date: March 1, 2026
Duration: 50 minutes
Location: Virtual (Google Meet)
Project: Client Discovery Call — UrbanBazaar Marketplace

Attendees:
- Lisa Wang (CEO, UrbanBazaar — Client)
- Tom Bradley (Head of E-Commerce, UrbanBazaar — Client)
- Meera Singh (Business Analyst, Our Team)
- James Wilson (Sales Lead, Our Team)

---

James: Lisa, Tom — tell us about the initiative.

Lisa: UrbanBazaar is a curated marketplace for local artisans. We have 500
sellers and 20,000 monthly active buyers. Currently on Shopify but we've
outgrown it. We need a custom marketplace with multi-vendor support.

Tom: Main pain points: Shopify's commission structure is killing our margins.
We can't customize the seller onboarding. Search is terrible — our artisans
tag products inconsistently. And we have no recommendation engine.

Lisa: We need: multi-vendor marketplace with independent seller dashboards,
advanced search with filters (category, price, location, ratings), a
recommendation engine based on browsing history, and a review/rating system.

Tom: Payment is critical — we need split payments. When a buyer purchases from
3 sellers in one cart, the payment should split automatically. We use Stripe
currently. Also need support for discount codes and seasonal promotions.

Lisa: Mobile-first design. 70% of our traffic is mobile. Performance matters —
product pages must load in under 1.5 seconds.

Tom: For sellers: they need dashboards showing sales analytics, inventory
management, order fulfillment tracking, and the ability to run their own
promotions.

Meera: Any compliance requirements?

Lisa: PCI-DSS for payments obviously. GDPR because we have European buyers.
We need cookie consent management and data deletion requests.

Tom: Timeline: 4 months for MVP. Budget: $120,000.
Expected scale: 2,000 sellers and 100,000 MAU within a year.

Decisions Made:
- Phase 1: Multi-vendor marketplace, search, payments, seller dashboards
- Phase 2: Recommendation engine, mobile app, AI-powered tagging
- Stripe for payments with split payment support
- Mobile-first responsive design
