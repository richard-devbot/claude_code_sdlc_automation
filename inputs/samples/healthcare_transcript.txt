Anita (Sales): Thank you all for joining. Dr. Mehta, Priya — let's start with
what's driving this initiative.

Dr. Mehta: Our biggest problem is appointment no-shows. We're losing roughly
30% of appointments because patients forget. We need automated SMS and email
reminders — at least 24 hours before the appointment.

Priya: The insurance claim process is a nightmare. It takes 7 days to process
a claim manually. We want to bring this down to under 24 hours with automated
validation against insurance providers' databases.

Sai: So the two main pain points are: appointment no-shows and slow insurance
claim processing. What about the booking process?

Dr. Mehta: We need an online booking portal. Patients call in to book. We want
online booking, rescheduling, and cancellation. Doctors should manage their
own availability.

Priya: I need an admin dashboard for managing users and generating reports on
appointment volumes, no-show rates, and claim processing times.

Dr. Mehta: Security is critical. We handle PHI. We MUST be HIPAA compliant.
Every login needs MFA. We need audit trails for patient data access.

Vikram: Do you have an existing patient database?

Dr. Mehta: Yes, legacy PostgreSQL with 50,000 patient records. Migration needed
with zero downtime. We also have a Twilio account for SMS.

Dr. Mehta: We want to go live in 6 months. Phase 1: booking, insurance validation,
admin dashboard. Phase 2: telemedicine, advanced analytics, patient records.

Priya: Budget is flexible. We need a clear SOW before committing.

Dr. Mehta: 200 doctors, 5,000 active patients monthly, 50 admin staff.
Peak concurrent users: 500-1000.

Decisions Made:
- Phase 1: Booking, Insurance, Admin Dashboard, Notifications
- Phase 2: Telemedicine, Advanced Analytics, Mobile App
- HIPAA compliance non-negotiable
- 6 months for Phase 1
