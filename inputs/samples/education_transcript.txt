Duration: 55 minutes
Location: Virtual (Zoom)
Project: Client Discovery Call — BrightPath Academy

Attendees:
- Dr. Emily Foster (Director, BrightPath Academy — Client)
- Michael Torres (IT Manager, BrightPath — Client)
- Riya Gupta (Business Analyst, Our Team)
- Sam Mitchell (Sales Lead, Our Team)

---

Sam: Dr. Foster, Michael — what does BrightPath need?

Dr. Foster: BrightPath is a K-12 private school network with 5 campuses and
3,000 students. We're still using paper-based processes for enrollment,
attendance, and grade reporting. Parents call the front desk for everything.
We need a complete school management platform.

Michael: Our pain points: enrollment takes 2 weeks of paperwork. Attendance
is tracked on paper and entered into spreadsheets weekly. Report cards are
generated manually in Word. Parents have zero visibility into their child's
progress between report card periods.

Dr. Foster: We need: online enrollment with document upload, daily digital
attendance tracking, real-time grade book for teachers, parent portal with
student progress dashboards, and communication tools (announcements,
messaging between parents and teachers).

Michael: For teachers: lesson planning tools, assignment creation with
online submission, and a gradebook that automatically calculates weighted
grades. For admin: enrollment analytics, attendance reports, and financial
reports (tuition payments).

Dr. Foster: Accessibility is critical — WCAG 2.1 AA compliance. We have
students and parents with disabilities. FERPA compliance for student records.
Parents should only see their own children's data.

Michael: We use PowerSchool currently for basic SIS but it's outdated.
We need data migration for student records. Google Classroom integration
for assignment delivery. Integration with our existing payment gateway
for tuition.

Dr. Foster: 300 teachers, 3,000 students, 5,000 parents, 50 admin staff.
Timeline: ready for next academic year (4 months). Budget: $80,000.

Decisions Made:
- Phase 1: Enrollment, Attendance, Gradebook, Parent Portal
- Phase 2: Lesson Planning, Advanced Analytics, Mobile App
- FERPA and WCAG 2.1 AA compliance required
- 4 months for Phase 1
