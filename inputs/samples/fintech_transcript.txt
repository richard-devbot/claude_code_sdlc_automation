Meeting Date: February 20, 2026
Duration: 65 minutes
Location: Virtual (Teams)
Project: Client Discovery Call — PayWise Financial

Attendees:
- Robert Kim (CTO, PayWise Financial — Client)
- Anna Lopez (Compliance Officer, PayWise — Client)
- Dev Patel (Business Analyst, Our Team)
- Nisha Rao (Sales Lead, Our Team)

---

Nisha: Robert, Anna — what's driving this project?

Robert: PayWise is a lending platform. We process 500 loan applications daily.
Everything is manual — our underwriters review each application by hand, checking
credit scores, income verification, and risk assessment. It takes 3-5 business
days. We need to bring this to under 4 hours for standard applications.

Anna: From compliance perspective, we need full KYC/AML checks automated.
Currently our compliance team manually verifies identity documents. We need
automated document verification integrated with third-party KYC providers.

Robert: We need: automated loan underwriting engine with configurable risk
rules, document upload and OCR for income verification, real-time credit
score integration, customer portal for application tracking, and admin
dashboard for underwriters to handle edge cases.

Anna: SOC2 Type II is required. PCI-DSS for any payment processing. All
customer PII must be encrypted at rest and in transit. Complete audit trail
for every decision — regulatory auditors need to trace why any loan was
approved or denied.

Robert: Integration needs: Experian/TransUnion for credit, Plaid for bank
verification, DocuSign for loan agreements, and Twilio for notifications.

Robert: 200 internal users, 10,000 applicants monthly. Expecting 50,000
monthly within a year. System must handle burst traffic during promotions.

Anna: Two-factor auth mandatory. Session timeout at 15 minutes for admin.
Fraud detection for duplicate applications and identity theft.

Robert: 6-month timeline. $200,000 budget. Phase 1: core underwriting,
KYC, customer portal. Phase 2: AI risk scoring, advanced analytics.

Decisions Made:
- Phase 1: Loan underwriting, KYC/AML, customer portal, admin dashboard
- Phase 2: AI risk scoring, predictive analytics, partner API
- SOC2 and PCI-DSS compliance required
- 6 months for Phase 1
